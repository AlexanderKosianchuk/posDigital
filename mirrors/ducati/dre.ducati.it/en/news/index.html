DRE 2015 has begun!

Over the weekend of 8-10 May, the 2015 season of the Ducati Riding Experience officially got underway.

To ‘open the show’ was the Multistrada Tech Ride, one of the main new features introduced this year, and which saw participants, who were welcomed in the beautiful setting of the Hotel Borgo Tre Rose di Montepulciano in the province of Siena, learn the secrets of riding both on- and off-road with the Ducati multibike.
